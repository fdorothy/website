an early morning coffee,<br>
an early morning walk<br>
what journey shall we go on?<br>
what fruit shall we pluck?<br>

the wind blows the seeds<br>
that we cast without regret<br>
with hungry eyes we dig<br>
our toil's not done yet<br>

dirty worms writhing<br>
shifting through the mud<br>
fingers twist and turn the soil<br>
until the time has come<br>

the wind blows the seeds<br>
that we cast without regret<br>
with hungry eyes we dig<br>
our toil's not done yet<br>

seeds in hand he steps aside<br>
throws them to the wind<br>
lets them find their home<br>
lets them rise again<br>
