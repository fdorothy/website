Despite being a heavy metal nut, in college I obtained a mandolin and fell in love with playing celtic tunes. Over the past couple years I've picked up and have been learning spanish guitar.

Music has been a source of therapy for me. Being able to pick up an instrument and strum some chords or play through a few simple tunes has helped me get through some rough times. Using music as therapy is so important to me that I want to encourage others, especially my kids, to learn music so that they can find some comfort as well in dark times.
